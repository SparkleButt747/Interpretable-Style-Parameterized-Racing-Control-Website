## Why this model
We need fast, stable, legible dynamics so style changes show **cause → effect**. The **kinematic bicycle** plus a friction limit is the right compromise.

<Separator className="my-8" />

## Kinematic Bicycle — core equations

```math
\dot{x}=v\cos(\psi+\beta),\quad
\dot{y}=v\sin(\psi+\beta),\quad
\dot{\psi}=\frac{v}{L}\sin\beta,\quad
\beta=\arctan\!\Big(\frac{l_r}{L}\tan\delta\Big),\quad
\dot{v}=a_x.
```

## Friction & feasible speed

**Friction circle:**
$$
(a_x)^2 + (v^2\kappa)^2 \le (\mu g)^2 .
$$

**Curvature → target speed (then smooth):**
```math
v_t(s) \approx \sqrt{\frac{\mu g}{|\kappa(s)|+\epsilon}}.
```

## Inputs, Outputs, State

**State (to controller)**
- $(x,y,\psi,v)$, local $\kappa(s)$, lookahead index/arc-length

**Inputs (from controller)**
- Steering $\delta$, Longitudinal $a_x$

**Limits**
- $|\delta|\le \delta_{\max}$, $|\dot\delta|\le \dot\delta_{\max}$, $|a_x|\le a_{x,\max}$

**Telemetry**
- Trajectory, speed trace, lateral g $= v^2|\kappa|/g$, jerk, apex flags

<Separator className="my-10" />

## Smoothing & rates

- Low-pass or clamp $|\dot\delta|$ for steering
- Limit $|\dot a_x|$ (jerk) around $v_t(s)$ changes
- Optional: blend preview target to avoid curvature noise

## Limitations & realism

- No tyre relaxation, load transfer, aero, or thermal effects  
- Not a lap-time oracle; **is** a clarity machine  
- Built to teach preview, curvature→speed, rate limits, and **style**
