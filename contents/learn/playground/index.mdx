## Metrics (the “why” behind each)

- Lap time $T_{\text{lap}}$
- Lateral g (max/avg): $a_y = \frac{v^2|\kappa|}{g}$
- Mean jerk (proxy): average $|\Delta a_x|/\Delta t$
- RMS contouring error: $\text{RMSE}(e_\perp)$
- Overshoot count, apex hit/miss, off-track time
- Peak friction usage:

$$
\max \frac{\sqrt{a_x^2 + (v^2\kappa)^2}}{\mu g}
$$
