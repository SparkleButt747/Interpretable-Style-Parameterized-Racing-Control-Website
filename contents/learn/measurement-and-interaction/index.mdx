## Primary Metrics

- **Lap time** $T_\text{lap}$
- **Lateral g (max/avg)** $a_y = v^2|\kappa|/g$
- **Mean jerk** $\bar{j} = \frac{1}{T}\sum |\Delta a_x|/dt$
- **RMS contouring error** $\text{RMSE}(e_\perp)$
- **Apex hit/miss** (per corner) • **Overshoot count**
- **Control effort** $\sum \|\mathbf{u}\|^2 dt$ • **Rate penalty** $\sum \|\dot{\mathbf{u}}\|^2 dt$
- **Friction usage** $\max \frac{\sqrt{a_x^2+(v^2\kappa)^2}}{\mu g}$ • **Off-track time**

## Head-to-Head (Ghost Races)

- Opponents: **Baseline** preview, **MPCC-like** ghost  
- **Scoring**:
  - +3 (≥1.0 s faster), +2 (≥0.5 s), +1 (≥0.2 s) on $T_\text{lap}$
  - −1 if $\text{RMSE}(e_\perp) > 0.6$ m or any off-track
  - −1 if mean jerk > threshold (track-dependent)
  - Tie-break: lower **peak friction usage**
