## Experiments (guided)

### 1) Lookahead vs Oscillation — *S-bend*
- **Do this:** Sweep $L=2..20$ m; record $\text{RMSE}(e_\perp)$, overshoot, $T_\text{lap}$.
- **Expect:** small $L$ oscillates; mid $L$ stabilises; large $L$ sluggish.

### 2) Style Knobs & Apex Timing — *Hairpin*
- **Do this:** Vary Apex offset $[-2, +2]$ m and Entry/Exit bias $[-1, +1]$.  
- **Expect:** later apex (+offset) → better exit; entry-bias helps rotation but hurts exit.

### 3) Progress vs Smoothness Pareto — *Chicane*
- **Do this:** sweep $w_p$ vs Smoothness; log $T_\text{lap}$, mean jerk; plot frontier.  
- **Expect:** clear Pareto curve; aggressive settings buy small time for large jerk.

### 4) Low-μ Robustness — *All tracks*
- **Do this:** μ: dry→wet; minimal re-tune (↓Risk, ↑Smoothness).
- **Expect:** friction peaks drop; off-tracks go away with a modest lap-time tax

### 5) Controller Contrast (Ghost Race) — *Chicane*
- **Do this:** MPCC-like preset B vs Style-Param tuned late-apex/exit.  
- **Expect:** MPCC-like slightly faster; Style-Param smoother & more interpretable.
